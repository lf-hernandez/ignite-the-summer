The Format

Enlighten us — but make it quick

Ignite is a talk format built for momentum. Your slides advance automatically, whether you're ready or not. It's exhilarating, a little terrifying, and the most fun you'll ever have presenting.

5

Minutes total

Every talk is exactly five minutes. No overtime, no mercy, no rambling Q&A.

20

Slides

Exactly twenty slides. Big visuals beat bullet points — keep them punchy.

15

Seconds per slide

Slides auto-advance every 15 seconds. The clock is your co-presenter.

1

Big idea

One story, one lesson, one obsession. Depth over breadth — light the spark.

Topics

What should you talk about?

Anything that would make a room full of Orlando developers lean in. Technical deep-dives, career stories, spectacular failures, weird side projects — if it lit you up, it'll light us up.

AI & LLMs in the wild Web & frontend Cloud & DevOps Mobile Data & analytics Security Open source adventures Career & leadership Postmortems & war stories Side projects & hardware hacks Dev tooling Something we didn't think of

✨ First-time speakers are extremely welcome. Five minutes is the perfect first talk, and we'll help you rehearse before the big night.
